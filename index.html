Faith-filled games that point people to Jesus

Our Mission

The Thoughts & Prayers Foundation is a nonprofit public benefit corporation dedicated to creating faith-filled games that point people to Jesus — inspiring hope, building community, and encouraging players to grow in their relationship with Him.

About Us

We believe in the power of story and technology to share the love of Jesus in fresh, creative ways. Guided by the values of service, community, and imagination, we design interactive experiences that help people walk with Jesus every day and discover the joy of faith through play.
