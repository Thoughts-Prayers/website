Our Mission

The Thoughts & Prayers Foundation is a nonprofit public benefit corporation dedicated to developing Christ-centered video games that inspire, educate, and support spiritual growth in players of all ages.

About Us

We believe in the power of storytelling and technology to spread the Gospel. Our work is guided by the principles of service, discipleship, and creativity — with the goal of helping people strengthen their walk with Christ through play.
